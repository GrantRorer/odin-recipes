Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic italian dish. It's dough with tomato sauce on top of it, then a layer of cheese and any other toppings usually a protien of choice.

Ingrediants

      1 can (8 ounces) of Tomato Sauce-No Salt Added
      1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
      1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
      1 prebaked think pizza crust (12-inch)
      1 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
      1/3 cup sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Combine tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese and oregano in small bowl; spread tomato sauce evenly over crust. Sprinkle pizza with mozzarella cheese; top with pepperoni.
  2. Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust are browned lightly and cheese melts. Cut pizza into 6 slices.
